Virtual Nature The Game

On this adventure you will be exploring actual sights and locations of Koli!

You will also get some basic knowledge of Koli and it's surroundings.

Start your adventure by typing your name!

Note that this input will only accept alphabetical letters (a-z)! Max length 15 letters.

About this game

This game was made by Information Technology students of Karelia University of Applied Sciences.

Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome are currently known to support this game the best. Therefore it is highly recommended to use these browsers for the best performance.
